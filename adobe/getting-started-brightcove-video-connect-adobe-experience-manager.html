Getting Started with Brightcove Video Connect for Adobe Experience Manager

This topic provides an overview of Brightcove Video Connect for Adobe Experience Manager.
Notes:
  • Brightcove offers an onboarding package to help you with the initial installation and configuration of the connector - we strongly encourage you to take advantage of this to insure that the implementation goes smoothly. Contact your account manager for details.
  • Links to all releases and changelogs can be found on the Releases page.
  • Version 5.7 of the connector is now available with support for AEM Cloud as a Service.
  • Version 5.5.4 of the connector will no longer be supported as of October 1, 2022.
  • Support for AEM v6.2 ended as of January 1, 2021.
