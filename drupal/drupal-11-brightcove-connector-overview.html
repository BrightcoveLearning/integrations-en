Introduction

The Drupal-Brightcove connector for Drupal 11 enhances the previous version, enabling you to leverage the latest features of Drupal 11. While Drupal 11 may not introduce significant new functionalities compared to Drupal 9, it does offer substantial performance improvements and updates to underlying software subsystems. These enhancements contribute to greater security, stability, and maintainability of your Drupal environment.

Installation and documentation

To install the connector for Drupal 11, please follow the instructions on the Drupal site.
